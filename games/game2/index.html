Game Overview

Switchblade is an innovative action game that challenges players to master the art of switching between different abilities. Navigate through increasingly difficult levels while adapting your strategy on the fly.

Developed by a talented team including rubic, torcado, ShyCryptid, Camelot, and keestak, this game offers hours of engaging gameplay with its unique mechanics.

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Press Space to jump and double-jump
  • Use number keys 1-4 to switch between different abilities
  • Each ability has unique strengths - learn when to use each one
  • Collect power-ups to enhance your abilities
