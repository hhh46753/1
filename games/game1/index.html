Hextris

A fast-paced puzzle game based on hexagonal shapes that will challenge your reflexes and strategy.

How to Play Hextris

Hextris is an addictive puzzle game where you rotate a hexagon to match colors and prevent blocks from reaching the center. The game gets progressively faster and more challenging as your score increases.

Game Controls:

  • Use the left and right arrow keys to rotate the hexagon
  • Match three or more blocks of the same color to clear them
  • Prevent blocks from reaching the center of the hexagon
  • The game ends when blocks stack up to the center

About Hextris

Hextris is an open-source HTML5 game created by a team of developers as a modern twist on the classic Tetris concept. Instead of the traditional grid, Hextris uses a hexagonal playing field that creates unique gameplay mechanics and challenges.
